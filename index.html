Our Mission

is to power communities by promoting healthy, sustainable living. Our products allow everyone to grow their own food, right in their living rooms and kitchens. We believe in a future where people around the earth become producers instead of just consumers.

We're a team of engineers, designers, scientists and visionaries with a passion for enabling distributed production.

Cortex

The Cortex provides a complete solution for monitoring, tracking, and controlling key variables of any hydroponic garden or farm. Dose nutrients, adjust pH, and monitor and control resevoir and environmental factors right from the web or from any smartphone.

Microfarm

A beautiful, configurable home farming appliance roughly the size of a bookshelf that can produce a significant amount of fruits and vegetables right in any household. Welcome to the future; healthy,organic food grown right in your home.
